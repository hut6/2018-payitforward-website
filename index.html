Kind Space NT

Kind Space NT is the marketplace for acts of kindness in the Northern Territory.

Are you desparate for some help? Do you struggle to get things done? Are you or your family under financial strain and aren't coping with things?

Then Kind Space NT might be able to help.

Who can use Kind Space NT?

Here's who we can think of, but surely creative people will surprise us.

Patients

Do you have to attend regular appointments and are strapped for cash? For example, chemo therapy, dialysis, physio / occupational therapy, support group meetings? Then Kind Space NT can help you.

Teens

You don't have a car or a licence yet, and you struggle to get to sporting activities? You don't have anyone to take you? Then Kind Space NT can help you.

Job Seekers

You've just arrived in the territory, you're poor and you don't have a car yet. You have job interviews to go to. Then Kind Space NT can help you too.

Anyone in need

Are you under financial strain and aren't coping with things? There might be someone out there willing to provide a random act of kindness. Try Kind Space NT, you might be surprised.

Download Kind Space NT if you need help

Kind Space NT needs your help too!

There are people in need of help out there. You might have some spare time on your hands. Can you lend a hand?

This is a chance to meet new people too. Especially if you've just arrived in the territory.

With Kind Space NT, the acts of kindness are not necessarily random; you know that you're helping the community out.

Don't expect to make money out of it. Being benevolent and helping out your community makes you feel good. People in need are not expected to pay for the services, but can choose to cover the costs of fuel for example, or make a gift.

Where to from here?

The app is a prototype currently. It needs much more work to be production ready.

Funding?

Because it relies on the community members doing favours to other community members, there isn't a lot of money going around to maintain the app, and pay for the infrastructure to keep the service up and running. We don't want to charge for the use of the app. We could accept donations, but that might not go far enough.

Support from government agencies such as the NT Department of Health might be interested in the app, as it helps people get to health services. Similarly, the NT Department of Tourism and Culturemight also be interested to increase the involments of teens in events in the community.

To avoid abuse of the system, we would need to have a rating system in place. If someone abuses the system, they get banned. We think that verifying identities of users (for both users and givers) will also help stop abuse and trolls. There are automated services out there that can do this but it will come at an increased cost.

The technology

The phone app was built using the Ionic Framework (https://ionicframework.com/). It has a simple data store on a Vultr server (MySQL + nginx).

The data sets used

City of Darwin Open Data Hub - Parks and Facilities Application
It's used in the app to list community locations (eg sporting facilities, parks, etc...) when people need to arrange transport to a location, without having to enter an address.

National Health Services Directory (NHSD) (Point) 2017
When someone needs transport to a health facility, the app will let users pick a health facility from a list, instead of having to input an address.
